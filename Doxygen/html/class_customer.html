Detailed Description

Contains actions that occur when a customer orders a dish from a restaurant.

Referenced by RestaurantOwner with a high degree of integration. The suggested implementation is for the RestaurantOwner to facilitate most of the interaction and the Customer instance to perform smaller tasks.
