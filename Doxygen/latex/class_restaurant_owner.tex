\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Contains all methods for actions that occur by the owner of a restaurant for smooth operation. 

References \mbox{\hyperlink{class_customer}{Customer}} with a high degree of integration. The suggested implementation is to facilitate most of the interaction with a \mbox{\hyperlink{class_customer}{Customer}} instance. Contains additional (standalone) methods for general restaurant upkeep.
