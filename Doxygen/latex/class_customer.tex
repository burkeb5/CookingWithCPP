\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Contains actions that occur when a customer orders a dish from a restaurant. 

Referenced by \mbox{\hyperlink{class_restaurant_owner}{Restaurant\+Owner}} with a high degree of integration. The suggested implementation is for the \mbox{\hyperlink{class_restaurant_owner}{Restaurant\+Owner}} to facilitate most of the interaction and the \mbox{\hyperlink{class_customer}{Customer}} instance to perform smaller tasks.
